Who we are

Getting bored as you have arrived early before catching your flight, Train or bus to your destination city and want to get insights of the city hassle-free but could not do so as you have your heavy luggage and don’t trust cloak rooms available at train station or bus stand hence for customers feasibility ‘Bag2Bag’ is your friendly companion that allows users to book their luggage with a single tap on the app and the entire luggage will be hand picked up and delivered with full safety and security without any damage at any destination with in a city within a stimulated time as we believe “service delayed is service denied".
